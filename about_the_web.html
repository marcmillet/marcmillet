About this web-resume

The idea of making a web-resume came to my mind when I noticed that the process of finding a job is really boring and it’s not easy to outstand (I don’t really think I will with this web, but I had to, at least, try). Can you imagine what would happen if instead of a boring CV i handed out paper with a QR code to this web? Would be funny!

It also gave me the opportunity to continue to learn web-programminng (HTML5, JavaScript and CSS3). I had already build a website as a project in University, which allowed me to get a basis in web design and made me want to learn more.

I hope you enjoyed going through it as much as I did when building it.
